How to play

Notes of a melody fall down a row of lanes. Strike each note the moment its tile crosses the gold line at the bottom.

On a keyboard the lanes map left-to-right to home-row keys. Pick 4–7 keys in Settings (fewer is easier); Layout shows which keys, and Speed is separate. On a touchscreen, tap the lane.

A Perfect (dead on the line) beats a Good. Consecutive hits build a combo that multiplies your score. Miss, or hit an empty lane, and the combo breaks — miss too many and the light fades.

Held notes glow at the line — keep tapping that key while the tail falls to whammy it for bonus points and a small health trickle (it never breaks your combo).

Each song shows a 1–5 difficulty rating as a row of gold pips, sorted easiest to hardest; the top arrows flip between Classics and Originals (✦). Esc pauses. In Joker mode a banked suit (♥♦♣♠) lights on a lane key — press it in a gap to spend its power.
